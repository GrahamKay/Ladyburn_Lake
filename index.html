Ladyburn Lake

Ladyburn Lake is situated on land re-claimed from a former opencast mine

The mine closed in 1983 and the remaining pit was lined and filled with water.

A huge amount of work has been carried out to create a varied and beautiful country park.

There are boats - but not all the time
